About the Atelier

Clockwork Atelier is a studio practice centered on mechanical design, precision craft, and story-driven builds.

Each piece blends brass, steel, and reclaimed materials to create mechanisms that feel engineered, durable, and alive.

From restoration to new mechanisms, the focus stays on bold form, meticulous detail, and timeless character.
